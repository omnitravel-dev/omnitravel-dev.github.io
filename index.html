OmniTravel

a smarter travel site

How it works

OmniTravel is a travel service that allows you to enter up to 5 different locations in a specific area and will select the best hotel for you based on distances from each of your destinations, minimizing the travel time needed during your stay.



We keep your budget in mind

Our hotel finder asks for your budget in terms of dollar signs from $ to $$$$.

We use TripAdvisor for our hotel finder

TripAdvisor is a user-generated content travel site that is the largest in the world.

OmniTravel is global

Our site works with cities and hotels from around the world, as long as they are registered on TripAdvisor.
